Operational scope

Where does the company operate and what are its characteristics? This determines which frameworks are mandatory.

🌍 Geographic presence

🇧🇷 Operates in Brazil
Activates Lei 12.846, LGPD in the Cross-Framework engine
🇪🇺 Operates in the European Union
Activa GDPR, NIS2, EU AI Act, DORA (si es financiera)
🇺🇸 Operates in the U.S.
Activates FCPA, CCPA (California), SOX if publicly traded
🇬🇧 Operates in the UK
Activa UK Bribery Act (incluido en FCPA & Bribery)

⚙️ Company characteristics

📈 Publicly traded (NYSE / NASDAQ / Merval)
Activates SOX — mandatory financial reporting controls
💳 Processes card payments
Activates PCI DSS v4.0
🏦 Regulated financial entity
Activa COBIT 2019 + DORA (si opera en UE)
🦺 Sector with high workplace risk
Activates ISO 45001 — Construction, Industry, Energy, Mining, Health
🤖 Uses or develops AI systems
Activates EU AI Act
🌿 Significant environmental impact
Activates ISO 14001 + ESG

🚀 Steps to activate the client

    

      

        
1

        
Copy the block above and replace modulos_activos + onboarding in the file config.js of the client.

      

      

        
2

        
Add the entry for clientes.json al array clientes[] en data/clientes.json.

      

      

        
3

        
If the client has their own subdomain or folder, copy the base repo and point it config_file to the correct config.

      

      

        
4

        
Send the credentials to the client: URL, login email, and temporary password. We recommend requiring a password change on first login.

      

      

        
5

        
The client will only see the activated tabs. To add modules, edit modulos_activos in its config, or use this wizard again.
